We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how QR Code Scanner handles your data.

Information We Do Not Collect

How the App Works

  • No scanned content is transmitted to servers.

Permissions We Request

Data Storage

All data remains on your device. Uninstalling removes all data.
